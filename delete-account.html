Account & Privacy

Delete your Aurenna AI account

We’re sorry to see you go. Follow the steps below to permanently delete your account and associated data.

How to request deletion

  1. Send us an email from the address linked to your Aurenna account.
  2. Use the subject line “Delete My Account” and include: your name (optional) and a brief confirmation that you want your account deleted.
  3. We’ll verify ownership and process your request within 7 days.

Email request to contact@aurenna.app

If you can’t access the original email address, email us and we’ll guide you through alternate verification.

What gets deleted

  • Profile information (email, display name)
  • Saved tarot reading history and spreads
  • Subscription entitlements attached to your account (if any)

What we may retain

  • Payment transaction records required for tax, accounting, or fraud prevention (retained for up to 12 months)
  • System logs needed to maintain the security and integrity of our services

All retained data is stored securely and only for the purposes listed above.
